⚙️ How It Works

1

Preprocessing Filter

Converts input frame to grayscale, applies Contrast Limited Adaptive Histogram Equalization (CLAHE) to sharpen shadows, and performs adaptive dynamic thresholding.

2

Geometric Alignment

Locates document contours and performs a perspective transform to rectify document tilt and rotation, preventing projection errors.

3

Cluster & Cell Grouping

Clusters dots horizontally into lines, measures inter-dot spatial distance gaps dynamically, and groups columns into 2x3 cell coordinate matrices.

4

Tactile Decoder

Decodes spatial cells into standard Grade 1 characters, digits, capitals, and contractions, logging images and records directly to the server's history database.
